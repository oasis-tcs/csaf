### Registry for Vulnerability ID Systems

- A registry for vulnerability ID systems was introduced to standardize naming conventions.
- A governance process was established for registering ID systems and mapping values in CSAF.
- The TC voted to adopt standing rules regarding the governance of vulnerability ID systems. The motion carries.
