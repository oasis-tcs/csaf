  - Christoph agrees that it is a good idea to have fixed values.
  - Feng mentions the Provider Urgency field in CVSS v4.0 where the vendor can provide their opinion on if something is very important or not important.
  - Christoph will work with the editors, Thomas and Stefan, to work on an implementation of the `qualitative_severity_rating` and draft guidance.
